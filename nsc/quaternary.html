Quaternary Period (2.6 million years ago – Present)

The Quaternary Period is the most recent period and the period we are in. It consists of the Pleistocene and Holocene epochs. This period only takes up less than 0.1% of all geologic time.

Life Forms during this Period
Life forms during the Quaternary Period include early humans and modern civilization.
Geological Features during this Period
The Ice Age occurs during this period and this period is characterized by extensive glaciation and evidence of glacial erosion.
