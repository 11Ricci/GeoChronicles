Cenozoic Era

What is the Cenozoic Era and when was it?

The Cenozoic Era is the current Era we are in and the last of the 3 eras during the Phanerozoic Eon. This era started 66 million years ago until the present time and is known as the age of mammals.

What was the state of Earth's structure during the Cenozoic Era?

During the Cenozoic Era, Earth's structure went through significant changes with continents moving into their current positions. These significant changes included the breakup of Pangea, collisions that formed major mountain ranges, and the formation of the Atlantic Ocean.

What were the most prominent materials during the Cenozoic Era?

Prominent materials during this era included sedimentary rocks from rivers, lakes, and marine environment, coal, oil, and natural gas, glacial deposits from ice age activity, and fossils of mammals, early hominids, and modern plants.
